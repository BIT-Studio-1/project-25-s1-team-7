Backstory - You wake up in a dingy dungeon that was ruled by an evil sorcerer years ago, eventually overthrown by the King's Royal Guard, leaving the castle abandoned to decay. Legend has it the evil sorcerer survived and can be found in the darkest corners of the castle. Maybe that's who brought you here? But why? Who are you? Why would the sorcerer want to kidnap you?

Player is a the highest ranking Knight in the King's Royal Guard, highly trusted by the entire Royal Family. The evil sorcerer is trying to rise to power once more, he has kidnapped the player to send shockwaves through the kingdom and weaken the Royal Guard, who are they without their Commander?

NPC could be an old Minion or apprentice of the evil sorcerer, locked up in the same dungeon for not wanting to help the sorcerer rise up again, who gives you backstory and insight into how to escape, he knows the castle like the back of his hand.
